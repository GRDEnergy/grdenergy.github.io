Services

COMMISSIONING SERVICES Cx

Building commissioning is a disciplined process aiming to optimize the energy performance and conditioned environment. We provide Cx services for all types of facilities including CALGreen and LEED projects.

RETRO COMMISSIONING SERVICES RCx
Retro Commissioning reduces consumption while improving the conditioned environment for existing buildings. We work with owners and utilities to provide and implement measures targeting the desired return of investment.

MONITOR BASED COMMISSIONING MBCx
Monitor Based Commissioning keeps going where Cx and RCx projects end, continually improving and increasing sustainability with targeted monitoring of all energy systems. We work with owners and utilities to provide all services for required for a successful MBCx program.

CONSULTING

CONTROL SYSTEMS BMS/FMCS
We provide design; implementation and verifications services for BMS and FMCS systems. Services include providing integration support for owners choosing to integrate different systems onto one platform or migrating systems from one systems to a new platform. Our services range from writing RFP’s to design assistance and implementation services, including commissioning of installed systems.

ENERGY INFORMATION SYSTEMS EIS

We provide design, implementation and verification services for EIS and building analytics systems. We can assist owners and consultants to choose the best available system for their application. Our services range from writing RFP’s to design assistance and implementation services, including commissioning of installed systems.
